Who is Ashlyn Pinter?

I am a lover of all things beautiful. I write poetry and I try to capture every moment in pictures or words. I am an aspiring author, and am currently putting together a book of poetry that I have written over the past few years. Most of the poems I write are free form and pertain to universal things such as the sun, the moon, and the stars.

If you'd like to dig into the depths of my mind follow my concious stream of thoughts represented in pictures via Tumblr.

Also find out more about me on other social media such as my instagram. Shoot a request and I'll open the gates to my world.
