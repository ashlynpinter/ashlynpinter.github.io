For Your Mind and Soul

Please enjoy these original poems by me. By sharing my work with the world I hope you respect that they are in fact mine and credit me whenever they are used. Don't forget you can find my blog with other orignial poems under the contact tab! Thanks so much and enjoy!

1.

The world is made up of tiny things
like the stars and how my heart jumps when the phone rings
like the way the light comes through the window pane or the way you speak so softly when you say my name
like the silent wings of a bird when it flys
or the way i lose myself in you bright brown eyes
like a meteor shower in the sky or the way you drag your hand across my thigh
its the little things like this that make me whole,
its the shooting stars and the roughness of your hands that make up my soul
-A.P.
