For Your Mind and Soul

Please enjoy these original poems by me. By sharing my work with the world I hope you respect that they are in fact mine and credit me whenever they are used. Don't forget you can find my blog with other orignial poems under the contact tab! Thanks so much and enjoy!

1.

The world is made up of tiny things
like the stars and how my heart jumps when the phone rings
like the way the light comes through the window pane or the way you speak so softly when you say my name
like the silent wings of a bird when it flys
or the way i lose myself in you bright brown eyes
like a meteor shower in the sky or the way you drag your hand across my thigh
its the little things like this that make me whole,
its the shooting stars and the roughness of your hands that make up my soul
-A.P.

2.

Memories are matters of the heart not the mind,
Heart beating rampant but my mind lags behind.
I dont know you in my head because I feel you in my heart
Pumping you through my veins up to my brain you turn my verses into art.
-A.P.

3.

You live by the sun,
You die by the moon
but I find otherwise..
Being tired is not something the sun hopes for you.
Only the moon wishes you softly to sleep
Exhaustion you acquire whilst the sun beats its harsh rays down upon you
Sandmen sprinkle sleep for you while kisses send from the moon.
With the day beating its rays and the night sending a cool breeze,
I rather live by the moon, than die by the sun. -A.P.

4.

The first rain of april will soon fall from the sky
The raindrops will know no difference between kissing the dirt or falling upon the strong leaves of an oak tree,
For the rain nourishes all that it touches without inhibitions.
I wish we could all go around kissing freely and giving love to those that have raindrops fall from their eyes,
and to those that do not know what nourishment they give.
I want to show them what strength they've built out of their weakness,
A fragile seed that's grown into a tree bearing fruit.
The next rain that will fall will not be from your eyes,
but from the skies,
falling upon you and kissing your skin until you see what strong leaves they've given you.
-A.P.
